Scenario examples

Practise before you're
standing in the room

Each scenario gives you a situation, choices and a clear safe response — so you're never caught off guard.

MSK

Patient reports sudden severe back pain

Learn the red flag signs that require immediate escalation and how to communicate this to your educator.

Respiratory

SpO₂ drops during treatment

Practise recognising the threshold at which to stop, call for help and what to document.

Neuro

Patient becomes confused mid-session

Understand AVPU and your safe first action when a patient's consciousness changes.

Elderly care

Patient refuses treatment

Learn to handle consent, respect autonomy and document clearly without panicking.

Community

Home visit — unsafe environment

Know when and how to raise a safeguarding concern on community placement.

Paediatric

Child becomes distressed during assessment

Practise child-appropriate communication and when to pause and involve a parent or guardian.
